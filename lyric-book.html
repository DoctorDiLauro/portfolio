--- layout: piece-page title: Chris Di Lauro • Lyric Book ---

Analyzing the artwork used by the band I found patterns. Hand made elements dominate their visual language – collages and fonts created with a childlike tone. Abstract uses of familiar imagery is also predominant.

From there, I planned out the features of the book. Hand rendered titles, collages serving as visual support, and hand distorted text within the lyrics to visually express the odd ways in which the vocalists use their voices.

Tasked with creating a quote book, I chose to put together a small selection of lyrics from the band Animal Collective. I aimed to create this book keeping in mind the band's unique visual and musical voice.
